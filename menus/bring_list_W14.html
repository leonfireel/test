Weekly Recipe (schema.org)

Ingredients

  • mixed baby greens (150g)
  • cherry tomatoes (300g)
  • mushrooms (300g)
  • spinach (200g)
  • arugula (100g)
  • broccolini (1 bunch)
  • carrots (4 units)
  • cucumber (2 units)
  • avocado (3 units)
  • bell peppers (2 units)
  • peas (100g)
  • zucchini (1 unit)
  • eggplant (1 unit)
  • onion (2 units)
  • garlic (1 head)
  • green beans (200g)
  • duck breast (2 units)
  • shrimp (300g)
  • chorizo (100g)
  • parmesan (100g)
  • ricotta (250g)
  • gorgonzola (100g)
  • halloumi (200g)
  • butter (100g)
  • sourdough bread (1 loaf)
  • whole wheat tortillas (2 units)
  • pasta shells (200g)
  • couscous (200g)
  • brioche buns (2 units)
  • crusty bread (1 unit)
  • arborio rice (200g)
  • olive oil (1 bottle)
  • balsamic glaze (1 small bottle)
  • lemon (3 units)
  • tahini (1 small jar)
  • sriracha (1 small bottle)
  • mayonnaise (1 small jar)
  • marinara sauce (1 jar)
  • orange (2 units)
  • honey (1 small jar)
  • saffron (1 small packet)
  • Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (1 meal)
  • Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (1 meal)

Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Caprese Avocado Toast with Balsamic Glaze on Sourdough + Side of Mixed Baby Greens with Cherry Tomatoes and Olive Oil
  2. Monday dinner: Creamy Garlic Mushroom and Spinach Pasta with Parmesan and Side of Arugula Salad
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Grated Parmesan
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Duck Breast with Orange-Honey Glaze, Roasted Carrots, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Spicy Tuna and Avocado Wrap with Sriracha Mayo in Whole Wheat Tortilla + Side of Cucumber and Carrot Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Vegetable and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Marinara Sauce and Side of Garlic Bread
  7. Thursday lunch: Quinoa and Grilled Vegetable Salad with Lemon-Tahini Dressing and Crumbled Gorgonzola + Crusty Bread
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Ratatouille and Steamed Green Beans
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Halloumi and Veggie Burger with Tzatziki Sauce on Brioche Bun + Side of Sweet Potato Wedges
  10. Friday dinner: Shrimp and Chorizo Paella with Bell Peppers, Peas, and Saffron Rice
