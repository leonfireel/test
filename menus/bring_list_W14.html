Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Provençal Omelette with Ratatouille and Side of Mixed Greens + Whole Grain Bread
  2. Monday dinner: Grilled Zucchini and Eggplant Tart with Goat-Free Pesto, Mozzarella, and Side of Arugula Salad
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Carrots and Green Beans
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Cod with Lemon-Caper Sauce, Sautéed Spinach, and Parmesan Polenta
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel-Free Chickpea Burger with Avocado, Tomato, and Tahini Sauce on Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Pork Tenderloin with Mustard-Honey Glaze, Roasted Asparagus, and Wild Rice Pilaf
  7. Thursday lunch: Greek-Style Orzo Salad with Cherry Tomatoes, Cucumber, Kalamata Olives, and Grilled Halloumi + Crusty Bread
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Saumon en Papillote (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Lemon-Dill Quinoa
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Portobello Mushroom and Mozzarella Panini with Pesto + Side of Carrot and Celery Sticks
  10. Friday dinner: Beef and Bell Pepper Stir-Fry with Black Pepper Sauce and Jasmine Rice
