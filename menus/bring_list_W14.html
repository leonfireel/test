Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Side of Arugula Salad (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  2. Monday dinner: Vegetarian Caprese Salad with Heirloom Tomatoes, Fresh Basil, Vegan Mozzarella, and Balsamic Glaze + Crusty Bread and Side of Quinoa
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Zucchini and Carrots
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Duck Breast with Cherry Sauce, Sautéed Green Beans, and Sweet Potato Mash
  5. Wednesday lunch: Lentil Burger with Avocado, Lettuce, Tomato, and Tahini-Yogurt Sauce on Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Cucumber Salad
  6. Wednesday dinner: Shrimp and Avocado Ceviche with Lime, Cilantro, and Red Onion + Side of Tortilla Chips
  7. Thursday lunch: Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap with Roasted Red Peppers, Spinach, and Chickpea Tzatziki + Side of Cherry Tomatoes and Quinoa
  8. Thursday dinner: Herb-Roasted Chicken Breast with Steamed Broccolini and Wild Rice
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna Niçoise Salad with Seared Tuna, Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Olive Oil Dressing
  10. Friday dinner: Pork and Apple Sausages with Caramelized Onions, Mashed Cauliflower, and Grilled Asparagus
