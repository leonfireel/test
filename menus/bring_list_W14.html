Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Vegetarian Croque Monsieur with Gruyère and Spinach + Side of Mixed Greens (Balsamic Dressing)
  2. Monday dinner: Zucchini and Ricotta Galette with Goat-Free Pesto, Cherry Tomatoes, and Arugula Salad
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Crusty Bread
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Mackerel with Lemon-Dill Sauce, Ratatouille, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel Burger with Tahini Sauce, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Carrot and Cucumber Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta with Parmesan and Side of Sautéed Spinach
  7. Thursday lunch: Tuna Niçoise Salad with Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Olive Oil Dressing
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf aux Carottes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Mashed Potatoes and Steamed Broccoli
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Halloumi and Watermelon Salad with Mint, Cucumber, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Pork Tenderloin with Mustard-Honey Glaze, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Sautéed Asparagus
