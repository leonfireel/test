Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Caprese Stuffed Avocado with Balsamic Glaze and Side of Mixed Greens + Whole Grain Bread
  2. Monday dinner: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Side of Roasted Cherry Tomatoes and Arugula Salad
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Grated Parmesan
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Duck Breast with Honey-Soy Glaze, Sautéed Bok Choy, and Jasmine Rice
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel and Hummus-Free Wrap with Lettuce, Tomato, Cucumber, and Tahini Sauce + Side of Carrot Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Shrimp and Zucchini Noodles with Garlic, Lemon, and Chili Flakes + Side of Crusty Bread
  7. Thursday lunch: Grilled Portobello Mushroom and Provolone Panini with Pesto and Side of Roasted Bell Peppers
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet à la Moutarde (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna and White Bean-Free Niçoise Salad with Hard-Boiled Eggs, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Pork Tenderloin with Apple-Cider Glaze, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Sautéed Spinach
