Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Caprese Stuffed Avocado with Balsamic Glaze and Whole Grain Toast + Side of Mixed Greens with Cherry Tomatoes
  2. Monday dinner: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Side of Garlic Sautéed Zucchini
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Grated Parmesan
  4. Tuesday dinner: Duck Breast with Cherry Port Sauce, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Sautéed Green Beans
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel Burger with Tahini Sauce, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Cucumber and Carrot Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Shrimp and Chorizo Paella with Bell Peppers, Peas, and Lemon Wedges
  7. Thursday lunch: Italian Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Red Onion, Capers, Olive Oil, and Crusty Bread + Side of Arugula
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Ratatouille and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Portobello Mushroom and Provola Panini with Pesto and Roasted Red Peppers + Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Thai Red Curry with Chicken, Pineapple, Bell Peppers, and Jasmine Rice
