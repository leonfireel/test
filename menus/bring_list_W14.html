Instructions

  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Grated Parmesan

  7. Thursday lunch: Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagel with Capers, Red Onion, and Side of Cucumber Salad
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Ratatouille and Steamed Haricots Verts
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Polenta with Roasted Bell Peppers, Olives, and Mozzarella + Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff with Egg Noodles and Side of Sautéed Spinach
