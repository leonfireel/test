Weekly Recipe (schema.org)

Ingredients

  • spinach (200g)
  • mixed greens (200g)
  • zucchini (2 units)
  • heirloom tomatoes (3 units)
  • basil (1 bunch)
  • broccoli (300g)
  • carrots (3 units)
  • cucumber (2 units)
  • eggplant (1 unit)
  • red onion (2 units)
  • cherry tomatoes (200g)
  • green beans (200g)
  • avocado (2 units)
  • cilantro (1 bunch)
  • duck breast (2 units)
  • shrimp (300g)
  • lamb chops (4 units)
  • ricotta (250g)
  • mozzarella (200g)
  • feta (100g)
  • butter (100g)
  • jumbo pasta shells (200g)
  • whole wheat tortilla (2 units)
  • tortilla chips (100g)
  • quinoa (200g)
  • couscous (200g)
  • crusty bread (1 unit)
  • whole grain crackers (100g)
  • hummus (1 small jar)
  • olive oil (1 bottle)
  • balsamic vinegar (1 bottle)
  • lemon (3 units)
  • lime (2 units)
  • garlic (1 head)
  • kalamata olives (100g)
  • capers (50g)
  • tahini (1 small jar)
  • mint (1 bunch)
  • Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (1 meal)
  • Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (1 meal)

Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Side of Mixed Greens and Balsamic Dressing
  2. Monday dinner: Caprese Salad with Heirloom Tomatoes, Fresh Basil, Mozzarella, and Crusty Bread + Side of Grilled Zucchini
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Carrots
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Duck Breast with Cherry Sauce, Sautéed Green Beans, and Garlic Mashed Potatoes
  5. Wednesday lunch: Grilled Vegetable and Hummus Wrap with Whole Wheat Tortilla + Side of Cucumber and Carrot Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Shrimp and Avocado Ceviche with Lime, Red Onion, and Cilantro + Side of Tortilla Chips
  7. Thursday lunch: Quinoa and Grilled Vegetable Bowl with Feta, Kalamata Olives, and Lemon-Tahini Dressing
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Ratatouille and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Red Onion, Capers, and Olive Oil + Side of Whole Grain Crackers
  10. Friday dinner: Grilled Lamb Chops with Mint Pesto, Roasted Eggplant, and Couscous
