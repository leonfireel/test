Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Crepes with Side of Mixed Greens (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  2. Monday dinner: Caprese Salad with Heirloom Tomatoes, Fresh Mozzarella, Basil, and Balsamic Glaze + Grilled Ciabatta
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Duck Breast with Cherry Port Sauce, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Sautéed Green Beans
  5. Wednesday lunch: Quinoa and Black Olive Burger with Avocado, Lettuce, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Carrot Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Japanese Teriyaki Salmon with Sesame Broccoli and Jasmine Rice
  7. Thursday lunch: Greek Orzo Salad with Cucumber, Cherry Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, Red Onion, and Olive Oil + Grilled Halloumi
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Rôti aux Herbes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Ratatouille and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Prosciutto and Mozzarella Panini with Arugula and Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto + Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Korean Beef Bulgogi with Kimchi and Steamed Rice
