Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Vegetarian Burger with Grilled Zucchini, Mozzarella, and Pesto Mayo on Brioche Bun + Side of Mixed Greens with Cherry Tomatoes
  2. Monday dinner: Ratatouille with Poached Eggs and Crusty Bread
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Haricots Verts and Grated Carrots
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce, Sautéed Spinach, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Prosciutto and Mozzarella Panini with Sun-Dried Tomatoes and Arugula + Side of Roasted Bell Peppers
  6. Wednesday dinner: Chicken and Mushroom Creamy Pasta with Side of Garlic Green Beans
  7. Thursday lunch: Greek-Style Orzo Salad with Cucumber, Cherry Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, Red Onion, and Feta-Free Lemon Dressing + Grilled Halloumi
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf aux Carottes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Hard-Boiled Eggs, Cherry Tomatoes, Red Onion, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Beef and Bell Pepper Stir-Fry with Soy-Ginger Glaze and Jasmine Rice
