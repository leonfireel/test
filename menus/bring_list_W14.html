Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Provençal Omelette with Ratatouille and Whole Grain Bread
  2. Monday dinner: Grilled Zucchini and Eggplant Salad with Burrata, Cherry Tomatoes, and Basil Oil + Grilled Ciabatta
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Grated Parmesan
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Cod with Lemon-Caper Sauce, Sautéed Spinach, and Garlic Mashed Potatoes
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel Burger with Tahini Sauce, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Carrot and Cucumber Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Vietnamese Summer Rolls with Shrimp, Rice Noodles, Mint, and Peanut Dipping Sauce + Side of Mango Salsa
  7. Thursday lunch: Italian Farro Salad with Roasted Bell Peppers, Artichokes, Olives, and Olive Oil + Grilled Halloumi
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf aux Carottes (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccolini and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap with Romaine, Parmesan, and Caesar Dressing + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  10. Friday dinner: Spanish Seafood Paella with Mussels, Shrimp, Bell Peppers, Peas, and Saffron Rice
