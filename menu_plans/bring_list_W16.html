Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Caprese Stuffed Avocado with Balsamic Glaze and Side of Mixed Greens + Whole Grain Baguette
  2. Monday dinner: Spinach and Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Side of Arugula Salad (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Zucchini and Cherry Tomatoes
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Duck Breast with Orange Sauce, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Steamed Green Beans
  5. Wednesday lunch: Portobello Mushroom Burger with Mozzarella, Roasted Red Peppers, and Pesto Mayo + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Japanese Teriyaki Salmon with Stir-Fried Bok Choy and Jasmine Rice
  7. Thursday lunch: Greek-Style Orzo Salad with Cucumber, Cherry Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, Red Onion, and Feta-Free Lemon Dressing + Grilled Halloumi
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Basquaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagel with Capers, Red Onion, and Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Italian Sausage and Bell Pepper Skillet with Polenta and Side of Sautéed Spinach
