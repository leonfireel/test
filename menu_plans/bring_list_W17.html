Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Provençal Omelette with Ratatouille Vegetables and Side of Mixed Greens + Whole Grain Baguette
  2. Monday dinner: Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa, Pine Nuts, and Mozzarella + Side of Arugula Salad (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Cannelloni aux Trois Fromages (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Spinach and Cherry Tomatoes
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Lamb Chops with Mint Yogurt Sauce, Roasted Eggplant, and Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Beetroot and Walnut Veggie Burger with Caramelized Onions, Swiss Cheese, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Thai Green Curry with Tofu, Bell Peppers, and Jasmine Rice
  7. Thursday lunch: Mediterranean Tuna Salad with Romaine Lettuce, Cherry Tomatoes, Cucumber, Red Onion, and Kalamata Olives + Whole Grain Bread
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf à la Bordelaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Halloumi and Watermelon Salad with Mint, Cucumber, and Balsamic Glaze + Side of Crusty Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Chicken and Mushroom Risotto with Side of Sautéed Zucchini
