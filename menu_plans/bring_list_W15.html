Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Egg and Avocado Sandwich on Whole Grain Bread with Side of Carrot and Cucumber Sticks
  2. Monday dinner: Ratatouille with Poached Eggs and Crusty Bread
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Zucchini and Cherry Tomatoes
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Mackerel with Lemon Butter Sauce, Steamed Green Beans, and Roasted Potatoes
  5. Wednesday lunch: Chickpea-Free Veggie Burger with Beetroot Patty, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Chicken Tagine with Apricots, Almonds, and Couscous
  7. Thursday lunch: Niçoise-Style Salad with Seared Tuna, Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, and Hard-Boiled Eggs + Whole Grain Baguette
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf Bourguignon (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Cheese and Tomato Sandwich on Sourdough with Side of Mixed Greens (Balsamic Dressing)
  10. Friday dinner: Pork and Apple Sausages with Mashed Cauliflower and Sautéed Apples
