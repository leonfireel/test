Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Vegetarian Croque Monsieur with Gruyère and Mushrooms, Side of Mixed Greens with Dijon Vinaigrette
  2. Monday dinner: Ratatouille with Poached Eggs and Crusty Bread
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Vegetable Lasagna with Side of Arugula Salad (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Duck Breast with Cherry Sauce, Roasted Potatoes, and Sautéed Green Beans
  5. Wednesday lunch: Beetroot and Goat Cheese-Free Burger with Walnuts, Lettuce, and Whole Wheat Bun, Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Grilled Mackerel with Lemon-Herb Butter, Quinoa, and Steamed Asparagus
  7. Thursday lunch: Melon and Mozzarella Salad with Basil, and Balsamic Glaze, Side of Grissini and Mixed Greens
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Basquaise with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna Niçoise Salad with Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Olive Oil Dressing
  10. Friday dinner: Pork Tenderloin with Mustard Cream Sauce, Mashed Cauliflower, and Roasted Carrots
