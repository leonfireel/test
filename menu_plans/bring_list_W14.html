Weekly Recipe (schema.org)

Ingredients

  • bell peppers (4 units)
  • mixed greens (200g)
  • cherry tomatoes (300g)
  • asparagus (1 bunch)
  • courgette (2 units)
  • peas (200g)
  • green beans (300g)
  • avocado (2 units)
  • lettuce (1 head)
  • tomato (2 units)
  • sweet potato (2 units)
  • arugula (100g)
  • spinach (200g)
  • bell peppers (3 units)
  • snap peas (200g)
  • mackerel (2 units)
  • chicken breast (300g)
  • beef strips (300g)
  • mozzarella (200g)
  • parmesan (150g)
  • cream cheese (150g)
  • quinoa (200g)
  • couscous (200g)
  • whole wheat buns (2 units)
  • wild rice (200g)
  • jasmine rice (200g)
  • whole grain baguette (1 unit)
  • arborio rice (200g)
  • scallops (300g)
  • smoked salmon (200g)
  • capers (50g)
  • olive oil (1 bottle)
  • lemon (3 units)
  • garlic (1 head)
  • croutons (100g)
  • Picard Hachis Parmentier (1 meal)
  • Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (1 meal)

Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Stuffed Bell Peppers with Mozzarella and Side Salad (Mixed Greens, Cherry Tomatoes, Olive Oil)
  2. Monday dinner: Courgette and Pea Risotto with Parmesan and Side of Grilled Asparagus
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Green Beans and Crusty Bread
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Mackerel with Lemon and Herbs, Ratatouille, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel-Free Chickpea Burger with Avocado, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Roasted Sweet Potato Wedges
  6. Wednesday dinner: Picard Lasagnes à la Bolognaise (Frozen Meal) with Side Salad (Arugula, Shaved Parmesan, Lemon Dressing)
  7. Thursday lunch: Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad with Croutons, Parmesan, and Light Dressing + Whole Grain Baguette
  8. Thursday dinner: Pan-Seared Scallops with Garlic Butter, Sautéed Spinach, and Wild Rice
  9. Friday lunch: Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Tartine with Capers, Red Onion, and Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Beef and Bell Pepper Stir-Fry with Snap Peas and Jasmine Rice
