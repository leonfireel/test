Weekly Recipe (schema.org)

Ingredients

  • bell peppers (4 units)
  • zucchini (3 units)
  • mixed greens (200g)
  • arugula (100g)
  • cherry tomatoes (300g)
  • green beans (300g)
  • eggplant (1 unit)
  • onion (3 units)
  • garlic (1 head)
  • carrot (4 units)
  • cucumber (2 units)
  • spinach (200g)
  • broccoli (1 head)
  • sweet potatoes (2 units)
  • kale (200g)
  • chicken breast (300g)
  • scallops (300g)
  • pork sausages (4 units)
  • mozzarella (150g)
  • ricotta (200g)
  • burrata (1 unit)
  • parmesan (100g)
  • butter (150g)
  • eggs (4 units)
  • quinoa (200g)
  • whole wheat buns (2 units)
  • ciabatta (1 unit)
  • whole grain bread (1 loaf)
  • couscous (200g)
  • polenta (200g)
  • falafel mix (200g)
  • tahini (1 small jar)
  • prosciutto (100g)
  • melon (1 small unit)
  • balsamic glaze (1 small bottle)
  • olive oil (1 bottle)
  • lemon (3 units)
  • capers (50g)
  • smoked mackerel (200g)
  • Picard Hachis Parmentier (1 meal)
  • Picard Boeuf en Daube (1 meal)

Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Stuffed Bell Peppers with Mozzarella and Side of Mixed Greens (Balsamic Dressing)
  2. Monday dinner: Zucchini and Ricotta Galette with Side Salad (Arugula, Cherry Tomatoes, Olive Oil)
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Crusty Bread
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Chicken Paillard with Lemon-Caper Sauce, Ratatouille, and Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel Burger with Tahini Sauce, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Carrot and Cucumber Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Seared Scallops with Garlic Butter, Sautéed Spinach, and Parmesan Polenta
  7. Thursday lunch: Prosciutto and Melon Salad with Burrata, Basil, and Grilled Ciabatta
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf en Daube (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Smoked Mackerel and Avocado Salad with Hard-Boiled Eggs, Cucumber, and Whole Grain Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Pork and Apple Sausages with Caramelized Onions, Roasted Sweet Potatoes, and Sautéed Kale
