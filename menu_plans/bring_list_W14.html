Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Egg and Spinach Muffin with Cherry Tomatoes, Mozzarella, and Side of Mixed Greens (Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing)
  2. Monday dinner: Ratatouille Niçoise with Poached Eggs and Crusty Bread
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Zucchini and Carrots
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Turkey Cutlets with Mustard-Honey Glaze, Roasted Potatoes, and Steamed Green Beans
  5. Wednesday lunch: Portobello Mushroom Burger with Caramelized Onions, Swiss Cheese, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Sweet Potato Fries
  6. Wednesday dinner: Thai Red Curry with Chicken, Pineapple, Bell Peppers, and Jasmine Rice
  7. Thursday lunch: Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Cherry Tomatoes, Cucumber, Red Onion, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Baguette
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf Bourguignon (Frozen Meal) with Side of Buttered Egg Noodles and Steamed Broccoli
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Halloumi and Peach Salad with Arugula, Walnuts, and Balsamic Glaze + Whole Grain Bread
  10. Friday dinner: Pan-Seared Cod with Lemon-Caper Sauce, Sautéed Spinach, and Herbed Couscous
