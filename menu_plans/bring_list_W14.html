Weekly Recipe (schema.org)

Ingredients

  • green beans (300g)
  • cherry tomatoes (300g)
  • olives (100g)
  • mixed greens (200g)
  • carrots (3 units)
  • lemon (3 units)
  • spinach (200g)
  • mushrooms (400g)
  • zucchini (2 units)
  • beetroot (2 units)
  • asparagus (1 bunch)
  • broccoli (1 head)
  • cucumber (2 units)
  • red onion (2 units)
  • turkey cutlets (300g)
  • cod fillets (300g)
  • beef strips (300g)
  • eggs (4 units)
  • gruyère (150g)
  • swiss cheese (100g)
  • gorgonzola (100g)
  • butter (150g)
  • whole grain baguette (1 unit)
  • whole wheat buns (2 units)
  • couscous (200g)
  • egg noodles (200g)
  • ciabatta (1 unit)
  • whole grain crackers (1 box)
  • crepes (4 units)
  • prosciutto (100g)
  • figs (4 units)
  • walnuts (50g)
  • tuna in olive oil (200g)
  • capers (50g)
  • olive oil (1 bottle)
  • dijon mustard (1 small jar)
  • Picard Hachis Parmentier (1 meal)
  • Picard Boeuf Bourguignon (1 meal)

Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Vegetarian Niçoise Salad with Hard-Boiled Eggs, Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, and Anchovy-Free Dressing + Whole Grain Baguette
  2. Monday dinner: Spinach and Mushroom Stuffed Crepes with Gruyère and Side Salad (Mixed Greens, Shaved Carrots, Lemon Vinaigrette)
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Sautéed Zucchini and Crusty Bread
  4. Tuesday dinner: Grilled Turkey Cutlets with Dijon Mustard Sauce, Ratatouille, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Portobello Mushroom Burger with Caramelized Onions, Swiss Cheese, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Roasted Beetroot Salad
  6. Wednesday dinner: Pan-Seared Cod with Lemon-Herb Butter, Sautéed Asparagus, and Garlic Mashed Potatoes
  7. Thursday lunch: Prosciutto and Fig Salad with Gorgonzola, Walnuts, and Mixed Greens + Grilled Ciabatta
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf Bourguignon (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Red Onion, Capers, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Crackers
  10. Friday dinner: Beef and Mushroom Stroganoff with Egg Noodles and Side of Cucumber Salad
