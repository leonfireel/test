Instructions

  7. Thursday lunch: Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad with Romaine Lettuce, Parmesan, Croutons, and Caesar Dressing + Whole Grain Baguette
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Poulet Basquaise (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Broccoli and Crusty Bread
  9. Friday lunch: Tuna Niçoise Salad with Green Beans, Cherry Tomatoes, Olives, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Olive Oil + Whole Grain Bread
