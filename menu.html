Instructions

  1. Monday lunch: Provençal Omelette with Ratatouille Vegetables, Goat-Free Cheese, and Whole Grain Toast
  2. Monday dinner: Grilled Zucchini and Aubergine Stacks with Mozzarella, Tomato Sauce, and Basil + Side of Mixed Greens (Balsamic Dressing)
  3. Tuesday lunch: Picard Hachis Parmentier (Frozen Meal) with Side of Steamed Green Beans and Crusty Bread
  4. Tuesday dinner: Pan-Seared Cod with Lemon-Caper Sauce, Sautéed Asparagus, and Herbed Couscous
  5. Wednesday lunch: Falafel Burger with Tahini Sauce, Lettuce, Tomato, and Whole Wheat Bun + Side of Carrot and Cucumber Sticks
  6. Wednesday dinner: Chicken and Chorizo Paella with Bell Peppers, Peas, and Saffron Rice
  7. Thursday lunch: Italian Tuna and White Bean-Free Salad with Cherry Tomatoes, Red Onion, Olives, and Olive Oil + Grilled Halloumi
  8. Thursday dinner: Picard Boeuf à la Mode (Frozen Meal) with Side of Roasted Carrots and Mashed Potatoes
  9. Friday lunch: Grilled Portobello Mushroom and Avocado Sandwich with Pesto and Mozzarella + Side of Mixed Greens
  10. Friday dinner: Thai Red Curry with Chicken, Pineapple, Bell Peppers, and Jasmine Rice
